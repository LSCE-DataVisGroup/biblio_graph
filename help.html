Description:
This application describes how the top 50 authors of the Ocean Acidification International Coordination Center (OA-ICC) group works together.
The bibliographic database of this working group that contains 3759 publications in date of the 31 of may 2016 has been analyzed to find the top 50 authors in number of publications. Each of them are represented by a node randomly colored with a size proportionnal to its number of publications. Nodes are connected by gray links that represent the number of co-written publication.

Click on a node to explore coauthors.
Once clicked on an author, a row chart that represents the different co-authors and their number of co-publications is display on the right side.
Double click anywhere on the graph layout to retrieve all connections.
